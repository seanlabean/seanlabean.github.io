Physics With Sean

Youtube - @physicswithsean
Instagram - @physicswithsean
Tutoring - physicswithsean.com

Pw/S is a video-based public education and entertainment adventure, growing out of the efforts of the ATAVATA project, and focusing on science communication and gaming. Physics was a natural place to start for me as I have the foundational knowledge and teaching experience. Astrophysics pop-science in particular appealed to me as it seemed to be a thriving community of educators, endless content, and a naturally participatory audience. The Pw/S content assembly line amounted to semi-daily Instagram/TikTok carousel posts on Astronomical content, weekly Physics Fridays problem solving videos, and irregular gaming and computer science livestreams.

As of 03A00 I have accrued over 150 subscribers and followers across all platforms.

manim

I designed, scripted, and recorded audio for the weekly physics problem solving videos using the Python library manim. Manim is purpose built for mathematic visualizations and animations. I encourage you, reader, to look at some example videos using the library, the extent of its performance is astounding. I managed to develop a rocky but functional system to quickly program animated problem statements and dynamic free-body diagrams. I had an astute sense that I had barely begun to scratch the surface and release the full power of manim. I did find it very well designed and convenient, especially in its ability to record voice-overs and then render select animations accordingly based on the duration of the audio. The learning curve is extremely steep, perhaps prohibitively so for those who do not already know how to program.

gaming

Computer games are obviously the odd-topic-out here, but nevertheless they make consistent appearances on the Pw/S channel. If anything, I enjoy recording the experiences for myself to review later, especially when the content is mostly just my brother and I chatting. Streamed games include Elden Ring and Outer Wilds.
