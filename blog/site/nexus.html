The nexus is a collection of topics I have spent significant personal time researching, usually as an effort to present the topic to others in presentations. These areas of interest are scattered, mostly unrelated to one another and ultimately do not further my career in any significant way but represent my ability to collect, condense, and communicate information.

Topics in the nexus range from how photography affected how artists paint lightning, to birds I see around Philadelphia, to my experience camping on a beach infested with wild pygmy horses. Each of these experiences or topics grabbed hold of my interest and I subsequently allowed myself to be enveloped by them, if only for a few days. As such, everything here more than deserves to see the light of day in some sort of preserved form. I hope you enjoy exploring them as much as I did.
